    Jade Cutting

    This process of transforming imperfect raw material into perfect pieces of jade is meticulous and requires years of experience to master. During the later stages, each jade slab is hand pressed against a rotary blade. Executing each cut requires extreme concentration and precision.

    Jewelry Making

    We partner with top jewelers who commit to exceptional craftsmanship, ethical practices and the finest materials. Each piece is handcrafted and made in small quantities to ensure that we never compromise our quality.

    Why Jade

    In the world of jewelry, jade is strongly associated in the Oriental culture with deep heritage roots. It is common for an infant to be raised wearing a piece of jade as it is believed to offer peace and protection to the body.
